What is Forex Comics?

A platform to buy and exchange second-hand and brand new foreign language comics .

We want to make learning a language fun.

Graphic novels and comic books are a wonderful way to learn a language - using rich images and conversational styles to entertain children and adults alike.

So whether you want to enjoy Tintin in the original French, or The Walking Dead in Spanish, Forex Comics provide a platform to improve your language skills and network with like-minded comic book fans.
