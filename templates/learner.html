Bored with course materials? Keen to learn a language faster and in a fun way?

Want to meet and practice speaking your target language with native speakers?

Forex-Comix provide a platform to enable you to do all the above.

The best part? It doesn't cost a single cent.

Exchange Meetups

Monthly meetings provide a wonderful opportunity for comic-book fans, and language learners to exchange comics, and converse in other languages

Social, casual and sharing a love for the written word, they take place in cafes around Dublin

It's also a great oppourtunity to make new friends.

On top of that, they're FREE to attend!
