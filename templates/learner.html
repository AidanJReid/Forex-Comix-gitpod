Bored with course materials? Keen to learn a language faster and in a fun way?

Want to meet and practice speaking your target language with native speakers?

Forex Comics provide a platform to enable you to do all the above.

Language learners can buy their chosen language comic directly from us, or participe in our monthly meetups and trade your comics/books with like-minded language learners.

Our meetups also allow you to socialise with other comic book fans (both in your native language and your target language).

Don't have any comics to trade with others? No worries! Check out our store to see what comics we stock and together we can make comics great again!

View Comic Store

Exchange Meetups

Monthly meetings are a wonderful opportunity for comic-book fans, and language learners to exchange comics, and converse in other languages

Social, casual and sharing a love for the written word, they take place in cafes around Dublin.

It's also a great opportunity to make new friends.

On top of that, they're FREE to attend!
